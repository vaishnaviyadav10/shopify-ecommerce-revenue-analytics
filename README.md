# Business Intelligence Enabled E-commerce Platform — Lovely Cakes

## 📌 Project Overview
Launched and managed a fully operational Shopify-based e-commerce platform ("Lovely Cakes") as part of a comprehensive Business Intelligence project, integrating active digital marketing funnels, real-time analytics tracking, and revenue optimization workflows.

---

## 🎯 Key Metrics & Financial Performance
* **Target Milestone:** ₹15,000 Revenue Goal.
* **Actual Performance:** Achieved **Subtotal of ₹17,267+ in total revenue**, successfully exceeding the target benchmark by **15%**.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Toolkit & Competencies
* **Core Architecture:** Shopify Platform, Shopify Analytics Dashboards
* **Business Intelligence Frameworks:** Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO), Funnel Tracking, Session Performance Analysis, Revenue Forecasting, Pricing Strategy Matrix
* **Growth Operations:** Data-Backed Upselling Strategies, WhatsApp Promotional Funnels, Customer Engagement Lifecycle Mapping

---

## 📈 Operational & Analytical Highlights
* **BI Dashboard Analysis:** Monitored live analytics counters to evaluate active web traffic sessions, user geographic distributions, checkout abandonment rates, and customer behavior trends.
* **Product Optimization:** Conducted detailed product performance and sales insights reviews to identify high-margin, fast-moving items, restructuring product presentation layers to prioritize conversions.
* **UX Funnel Refinement:** Maximized end-to-end customer conversion rates by simplifying navigation paths, deploying strategic add-on options at checkout, and streamlining the customer UX.
